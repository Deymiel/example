Our Team

We are inspired by our industry, craftsmanship, technology and high building development standards. We’re continually learning, and as a result, our team and capabilities now include forensic building defect inspections, expert reports, and waterproofing. Between us, we have more than 20 years’ experience in the construction and building industry.

STAN GIAOURIS

Managing Director/Founder/Building Expert

With over 20 years of experience in building construction, and being an award-winning building and construction consultant, Stan has expertise in:

  • Expert Witness inspection and reporting.
  • Waterproofing design and inspection.
  • General construction inspections and condition reports.
  • Remediation design solutions.
Resume Court Judgements

GEORGE NIKOLAS

General Manager/Senior Building Consultant

With over 20 years of experience in construction and building materials, design, manufacturing and supply, George specialises in construction advisory services, acting as a building consultant for owners’ corporations, clients’ representatives and project managers on behalf of property developers, builders, and home owners.
Competencies:

  • Construction detailing, products and code compliance, especially in regard to buildability and practical construction solutions.
  • Forensic and remedial building defect investigations across all aspects of construction.
  • Expert witness for defective building work with informed knowledge of the NCC and BCA.

NICKY EFTHIMIADES

Finance & Office Manager

Nicky plays a critical role in ensuring the financial health and stability of TCA, and in supporting its growth and success. With over 15 years of experience as Finance manager, Nicky specialises in:

  • Developing and implementing financial strategies
  • Budgeting and forecasting
  • Financial management/reporting
  • Overall office management

PRAMOD THEBE

Civil/Structural Engineer

An innovative professional at the forefront of technology with over 3 years of experience in Construction and remedial engineering. Pramod specialises in.

  • Engineering design and analysis.
  • Cost calculation and budgeting.
  • General Defect inspection and reporting.
  • Remediation solutions.

SULAV GAUTAM

Graduate Civil Engineer/Process Analyst

Sulav is responsible for designing, improving, and overseeing business processes along with performing general civil engineering duties such as preparing dilapidation report & condition report. With over 2 years of experience in process management, Sulav specialises in:

  • Designing and developing business processes;
  • Process improvement;
  • Process and data analysis;
  • General civil engineering duties.

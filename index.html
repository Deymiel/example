TRUSTED & EXPERIENCED

BUILDING & CONSTRUCTION

CONSULTANTS

Helping people navigate the complexities of building and construction is how we became The Construction Adviser. From expert building forensic reports to reviewing quotes to resolving conflicts through to ensuring timelines are met, we assess the quality and progress of projects as an independent representative to protect your interests.

QUALITY AND
PERFORMANCE

Our construction and building consultants are subject matter experts with long and established careers as building contractors, waterproofers, construction managers, and with manufacturers such as CSR and Strammit. Our specialised backgrounds and expertise enable us to support our insurance, construction, owner, and legal clients through damage assessments, cost estimating, valuation and pricing, cost of repair, code upgrades, betterment, and restoration. Having swung the hammer, overseen construction in the field, and/or managed contracting firms, our construction consultants have the requisite field experience across a multitude of construction industries from infrastructure, commercial, marine, and energy to provide you industry-leading expertise, support, and reporting.
